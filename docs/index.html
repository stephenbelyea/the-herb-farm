welcome to The Herb Farm

Thank you for visiting the online home of The Herb Farm!

We have recently moved from our old location in Norton, New Brunswick, to 149 Harper Settlement Road in Anagance, New Brunswick. We will be redesigning and updating our website in the coming months with new photos, news, and products available for our visitors.

You can catch more of Angela and The Herb Farm in the New Brunswick Craft Council's newest brochure (available at tourist bureaus throughout the province). Keep an eye out for products at the General Store Emporium in Sussex, New Brunswick, and at the Sussex Artist Co-Op. Starting this spring, you can also find Angela's goods at the Lotus Market (70 Record Street, Moncton, New Brunswick), and coming soon to the Artisan Village in downtown Moncton.

Angela is also a member of the Fundy Fibre Artisans craft group, a collective of local fibre enthusiasts sharing a passion for a sustainable lifestyle.

With all of this going on, it's hard to miss The Herb Farm in 2013!

Have a great day!

-Angela Belyea, Owner & Operator.

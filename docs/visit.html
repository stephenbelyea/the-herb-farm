pop by for a visit

Though not fully operational (or accessible) during the winter, we would love to have you for a visit any time of the year. You can usually find The Herb Farm on the King’s County Studio Tour or find Angela’s work at any number of locations around Southern New Brunswick.

If you’d like to check out Angela’s studio, peek into the greenhouse, peruse the gardens, walk the property and take a dip in the spring-fed pond – please stop by!

Just head over to our contact page and fill out the form and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
